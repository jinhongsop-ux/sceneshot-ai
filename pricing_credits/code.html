Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about credits and billing.

toll How are credits used?

Each generation typically costs 1 credit. High-resolution upscales or complex multi-subject scenes may cost 2-3 credits. You'll always see the exact cost before confirming a generation.

event_busy Do credits expire?

No, credits purchased through these packs never expire. They remain in your account until you use them, allowing you to generate at your own pace.

shield_lock How is my privacy handled?

Your generated scenes are private by default. We do not use your generated images to train our public models without explicit opt-in consent. Review our full Privacy Policy for details.

currency_exchange Can I get a refund?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for unused credits. If you haven't generated any scenes with a recently purchased pack, contact support for a full refund.
